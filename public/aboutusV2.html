About Us

Watany's Histroy Vision & Mission Board OF Directors CEO

Built on 2,336m2 on the outskirts of Cairo and at the joint route between the city and its newest suburbs, our new premises boasts 10 operation rooms, femtosecond laser machines in 2 refractive suites, 16 fully equipped examination rooms, and full investigative tools in a state-of-the-art eight-story building. Since its establishment in 2001 by Prof Riad Fikry, Prof Fathy Fawzy, and Prof Abdallah Hassouna, Al Watany Eye Hospital has taken solid steps toward establishing itself as one of the most trusted medical service providers in ophthalmology. In our dedication to excellence, we have successfully stepped into today’s high-tech culture by establishing a paperless operating system. Our homegrown electronic health records system encompasses outflow control across the hospital facilities and financial services as it integrates with clinical and affiliate remote service centers.

Values

– Harnessing all human and technical resources for serving patients.

– Achieving the optimum level of medical service and having distinct performance.

– Being honest with our patients regarding his/her health status.

– Creating a spirit of cooperation and affiliation among patients, employees, and doctors.

Mission

We provide one of the most distinct specialized high-quality medical services in Ophthalmology.Additionally, Research services and training programs for young ophthalmologists and staff are being applied to improve the quality of the provided service.

Vision

We aim for being one of the leaders in the scientific research and providing talented young ophthalmologists respecting the ethics of the profession to serve our country and anyone in need.
