Pharmacy

As getting your needed medicine is an essential part of your treatment journey. Watany Eye Hospitals was keen to provide specialized pharmacy services to our patients, by contracting with a group of pharmacies that are fully equipped to meet all their medical needs of local and imported medicines to serve all branches, and to provide our patients and employees with all types of medicines. The pharmacies operating in the branches of the Watany Eye Hospitals follow professional standards, as they operate with the highest standards of medicines supply and storage to ensure the availability of your medicines whenever you need them.
