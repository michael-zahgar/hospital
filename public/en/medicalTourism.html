Medical Tourism
at Watany Eye Hospitals

Watany Eye Hospitals is a leading medical institution specializing in eye care and ophthalmic surgeries, located in a prime location that offers a perfect blend of medical expertise and hospitality. We take pride in our state-of-the-art facilities, highly skilled doctors, and a commitment to providing exceptional care to patients seeking various eye treatments, including LASIK, Femto-LASIK, and blepharoplasty. In addition to our medical services, we strive to ensure that patients and their accompanying persons have a comfortable and hassle-free experience during their stay, with convenient accommodation options, transportation facilities, and on-site amenities.

LASIK and Femto-LASIK Services

Watany Eye Hospitals is renowned for its expertise in LASIK and Femto-LASIK procedures, which are popular among medical tourists seeking vision correction surgeries. Our team of experienced ophthalmologists, equipped with cutting-edge technology, performs these procedures with precision and care. LASIK and Femto-LASIK treatments offered at our hospitals are safe, effective, and tailored to meet individual patient needs. We prioritize patient comfort, ensuring a smooth and seamless process from the initial consultation to post-operative care.

Blepharoplasty Services

Watany Eye Hospitals also specializes in blepharoplasty, a surgical procedure aimed at enhancing the appearance of the eyelids. Our skilled plastic surgeons provide comprehensive assessments and customized treatment plans to address various concerns related to excess skin, fat deposits, and muscle laxity around the eyes. Whether patients require upper eyelid surgery, lower eyelid surgery, or a combination of both, our team delivers exceptional results with a focus on natural-looking outcomes and patient satisfaction.

Why Watany For Medical Tourism

Accommodation

We understand the importance of comfortable accommodation for patients and their companions. Watany Eye Hospitals collaborates with reputable hotels and resorts in the vicinity, offering a range of options to suit different preferences and budgets. These accommodations are conveniently located and provide a tranquil environment conducive to relaxation and recovery. Our dedicated staff can assist in making reservations and ensuring that patients receive special rates and amenities during their stay.

Transportation

Watany Eye Hospitals aims to make travel arrangements as convenient as possible for medical tourists. We provide assistance in arranging airport transfers, ensuring seamless transportation from the airport to the hospitals or chosen accommodation. Additionally, we offer transportation services for patients requiring post-operative follow-up visits or other related appointments. Our team coordinates these arrangements, prioritizing comfort, safety, and punctuality.

At Watany Eye Hospital, we strive to create an environment that promotes healing and tranquility. Our facilities are designed to cater to the needs of patients and provide a comfortable experience throughout their journey. Key features of our facilities include:
