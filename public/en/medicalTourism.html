Medical Tourism at Watany Eye Hospitals

At Watany Eye Hospitals, the best medical services are provided by the best, most experienced and highly qualified professionals.

Objectives & Goals of Watany Eye Hospitals:

Using the best and latest technologies to help provide all treatment options for patients seeking medical care overseas, such as the Middle East, Africa, China and Eastern Europe.

Watany Eye Hospitals have a distinguished location in terms of their proximity to the airport (especially Thawra and Hurghada branches) which facilitates the easy arrival of our international patients.

Ensure that patients receive the best possible health care while in Egypt.

Services to our International Patients
Transportations from the airport and to the hospital
Hotel accommodations
Assistance with flight bookings
Translation services
A carefully selected group of medical tourism staff to help schedule your appointments, all under one roof
Assistance with international insurance companies
Distinguished tourism program allover Egypt
Estimating the cost of services and assistance with payment
Provide patients with a detailed list of management options and their respective cost to help them determine the most appropriate solution
