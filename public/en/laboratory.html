As part of Watany Eye Hospitals’ keenness to provide the best medical service and facilitate our valued customers, Watany Eye Hospitals has contracted with Al Mokhtabar Lab to perform all medical analyses and examinations.

About

Al Mokhtabar Lab

All Eye Care Services in One Place

Al Mokhtabar is the largest private medical laboratory testing company in the Middle East, providing diagnostic laboratory services and pathological and clinical tests for medical communities in Egypt.

The company offers a portfolio of over 1,400 clinical analyses in immunology, hematology/coagulation, clinical chemistry, parasitology, microbiology/infectious diseases, toxicology, cytology, surgical pathology, flowcytometry, molecular biology, and cytogenetics. In the clinical analysis segment, samples are collected in more than 214 branches covering governorates of all areas in Egypt and analyzed in 2 central laboratories at the main governorates of Egypt.

Al Mokhtabar tests inside Watany Eye Hospitals

  • • Comprehensive examination test group

  • • Diabetes test kit

  • • Pre-operative test group

  • • Immunology test group

  • • Allergy test group

  • • Blood lipid test group

  • • kidney and liver function test group

  • • blood flow and hematology test group

  • • measuring therapeutic drugs test group

  • • Genetic test group

And over than 1,400 clinical analyses in the areas of immunology, hematology/coagulation, clinical chemistry, parasitology, microbiology/infectious diseases, toxicology, cytology, surgical pathology, flowcytometry, molecular biology and cytogenetics.
