Contact Lens

Most contact lenses are used to correct vision problems, such as nearsightedness (myopia), farsightedness (hyperopia), astigmatism, and vision problems related to aging. Lenses may also be used for cosmetic purposes.

“You can buy your contact lenses from a place you trust.”

Types of contact lenses

1-Colored lenses:

  • • Daily lenses
  • • Monthly lenses
  • • Extended lenses (yearly)

2-Therapeutic lenses:

  • • Cosmetic lenses
  • • Keratoconus lenses (American - Egyptian)
  • • Lenses for children

3-Transparent lenses:

  • • Daily lenses
  • • Monthly lenses
  • • Extended lenses (yearly)
  • • Astigmatism lenses
  • • Multifocal lenses (for reading)

Companies dealing with us:

The best brands in disposable contact lenses, with a wide choice of powers and durations to suit your lifestyle.
